# Decision Log

Record significant legal decisions here. Each entry captures what was decided, why, and context for future reference. This log helps Counsel OS recognize patterns and provide consistent advice.

## Entry Format

```
### [DATE] — [Brief Decision Title]
**Matter:** [counterparty/deal name]
**Decision:** [What was decided]
**Rationale:** [Why — the reasoning behind the decision]
**Alternatives considered:** [What other options were on the table]
**Risk accepted:** [Any risk that was consciously accepted]
**Decided by:** [Who made the call]
**Outcome:** [Result, if known — added later]
```

---

## Example Entry

### 2025-01-15 — Accepted 6-Month Liability Cap for DataCo Deal
**Matter:** DataCo / Project Falcon SaaS Agreement
**Decision:** Accepted a 6-month liability cap (below our 12-month standard) in exchange for a 3-year term commitment with 15% annual price escalator.
**Rationale:** The guaranteed revenue from the 3-year term ($2.4M total) outweighed the reduced liability protection. DataCo is a low-risk counterparty with no litigation history. Risk exposure at 6-month cap is approximately $400K.
**Alternatives considered:** (1) Hold firm at 12 months and risk losing the deal; (2) Accept 9 months as a compromise.
**Risk accepted:** Reduced recovery ceiling in the event of a material breach by DataCo. Mitigated by their strong financial position and our cyber insurance coverage.
**Decided by:** Sarah Chen, GC
**Outcome:** Deal closed 2025-01-22. Contract performing well as of last review.
