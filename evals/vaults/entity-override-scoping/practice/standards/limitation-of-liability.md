# Limitation of Liability

## Our Position

**Our standard:** When we are the customer, mutual cap on direct damages at twelve (12) months of fees. When we are the vendor, the same 12-month mutual cap is our ask.

**We'll accept:** Up to eighteen (18) months of fees for strategic deals, with internal sign-off.

**We won't accept:** Caps at twenty-four (24) months or higher, or uncapped direct liability — exposure is disproportionate to deal economics at our contract sizes.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Mutual cap at 12 months of fees.
- **YELLOW**: Cap between 12 and 18 months — needs sign-off.
- **RED**: Cap at 24 months or above, uncapped direct liability, or one-sided cap against us.

## Practical Guidance

Counterparty-specific concessions that were accepted in past deals are recorded in that counterparty's entity file and apply ONLY to that counterparty. They are not precedent for other deals.
