# Limitation of Liability

## Our Position

**Our standard:** Every services agreement must contain an express limitation-of-liability structure: a mutual cap on direct damages at twelve (12) months of fees, a mutual consequential-damages waiver, and carve-outs (uncapped or super-capped) for indemnification obligations, confidentiality breach, and data protection breach.

**We'll accept:** Caps between 12 and 24 months, or a 2x super-cap in lieu of uncapped carve-outs.

**We won't accept:** An agreement that is silent on limitation of liability. Silence does not favor us — it leaves remedies to background contract law, gives us no consequential-damages waiver to rely on, and forfeits the carve-out architecture that protects our data and indemnity recovery.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Mutual 12-month cap with the standard carve-outs.
- **YELLOW**: Cap between 12 and 24 months, or super-capped carve-outs.
- **RED**: No limitation-of-liability provision in the agreement, a one-sided cap, or carve-outs absent.
