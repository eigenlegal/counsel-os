# Indemnification

## Our Position

**Our standard:** As customer, we require the vendor to indemnify, defend, and hold us harmless against third-party claims arising from (a) the vendor's infringement of intellectual property rights, and (b) the vendor's breach of its data protection obligations. IP indemnity carries a 2x super-cap; other indemnities sit inside the liability cap carve-out structure.

**We'll accept:** Mutual indemnification with enumerated triggers, or vendor IP-only indemnity for low-risk deals under $100K with no data access.

**We won't accept:** An agreement with no indemnification provision at all, or one-way indemnification running against us.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Vendor IP and data-breach indemnity, structured per the cap carve-outs.
- **YELLOW**: Vendor IP-only indemnity on a low-risk deal.
- **RED**: No indemnification provision in the agreement, or indemnity one-way against us.
