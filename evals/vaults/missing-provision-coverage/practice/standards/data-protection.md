# Data Protection

## Our Position

**Our standard:** Any vendor that hosts, stores, or analyzes personal data — including end-user account and transaction records — executes our data processing agreement: processing on our instructions only, breach notice within 48 hours of discovery, sub-processor disclosure, deletion or return on termination, and a security exhibit.

**We'll accept:** Vendor-paper data terms of equivalent substance, reviewed against the same checklist.

**We won't accept:** A vendor processing personal data under an agreement with no data protection terms, or vendor rights to use personal data for its own purposes.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Our DPA (or equivalent) executed with security exhibit.
- **YELLOW**: Equivalent substance, security terms by reference.
- **RED**: Personal data processed with no data protection terms in the agreement, or vendor own-use rights.
