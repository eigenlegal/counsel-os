# Master Services Agreement (Davenport draft v1 — received 2026-06-08)

**MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT**

This Master Services Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into as of the date of last signature below (the "Effective Date") by and between Davenport Analytics Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales ("Provider"), and Northgate Commerce, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Customer").

**1. Services.** Provider will provide Customer with access to Provider's hosted analytics platform, which ingests, hosts, and analyzes Customer Data — including end-user account and transaction records supplied by Customer — and generates reporting dashboards and exports (the "Services"). "Customer Data" means all data submitted to the Services by or on behalf of Customer.

**2. Fees; Payment.** Customer shall pay the fees set forth in Exhibit A. Provider will invoice quarterly in advance; Customer shall pay undisputed invoices within thirty (30) days of the invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month or the maximum lawful rate, whichever is less.

**3. Term; Termination.** This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of one (1) year, automatically renewing for successive one-year terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days before the end of the then-current term. Either party may terminate for material breach not cured within thirty (30) days of written notice.

**4. Confidentiality.** Each party will protect the other party's Confidential Information with at least the degree of care it uses for its own similar information, and not less than reasonable care, and will use it only to perform under this Agreement. These obligations do not apply to information that is publicly available, independently developed, or rightfully received from a third party, and survive for three (3) years after termination.

**5. Intellectual Property.** Provider retains all right, title, and interest in the Services and Provider's pre-existing technology. Customer retains all right, title, and interest in Customer Data. Customer grants Provider a non-exclusive license to use Customer Data solely to provide the Services to Customer during the term.

**6. Warranties; Disclaimer.** Provider warrants that the Services will conform in all material respects to the applicable documentation. EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH IN THIS SECTION, THE SERVICES ARE PROVIDED "AS IS" AND PROVIDER DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, AND NON-INFRINGEMENT.

**7. Assignment.** Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other party's prior written consent, except to an affiliate or in connection with a merger or sale of substantially all assets.

**8. Governing Law.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to conflicts-of-law rules. The state and federal courts located in Delaware have exclusive jurisdiction.

**9. Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with Exhibit A, is the parties' entire agreement regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
