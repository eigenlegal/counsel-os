# Fees & Payment

## Our Position

**Our standard:** Customer pays undisputed invoices net thirty (30) days from invoice date. Late charges at 1.5% per month or the maximum lawful rate.

**We'll accept:** Up to net sixty (60) days for enterprise accounts — many large customers run fixed AP cycles, and the working-capital cost is priced in.

**We won't accept:** Terms beyond net 60, pay-when-paid constructions, or unilateral setoff rights.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Net forty-five (45) days or shorter.
- **YELLOW**: Net forty-six (46) to net sixty (60) days — note it in the matter.
- **RED**: Beyond net sixty (60), pay-when-paid, or unilateral setoff.
