# Indemnification

## Our Position

**Our standard:** As vendor, we indemnify the customer for third-party claims arising from (a) our infringement of third-party intellectual property rights, and (b) our gross negligence or willful misconduct. Indemnification obligations are subject to the limitation of liability, except IP infringement carries a 2x super-cap.

**We'll accept:** Adding third-party claims arising from our breach of confidentiality to the enumerated triggers, capped the same way.

**We won't accept:** Indemnification triggered by "any breach of this Agreement" (it converts every contract claim into an indemnity), or indemnification obligations excluded from the limitation of liability.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Enumerated triggers (IP, gross negligence/willful misconduct), subject to the cap with the IP super-cap.
- **YELLOW**: Enumerated triggers plus confidentiality breach, capped.
- **RED**: "Any breach" triggers, uncapped indemnification, or indemnity carved out of the limitation of liability.
