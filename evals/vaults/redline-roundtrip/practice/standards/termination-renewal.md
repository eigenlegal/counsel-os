# Termination & Renewal

## Our Position

**Our standard:** One-year terms with automatic renewal. Non-renewal notice of sixty (60) days before the end of the then-current term — we want runway to save the account.

**We'll accept:** Non-renewal notice down to thirty (30) days. Termination for material breach with a 30-day cure period.

**We won't accept:** Non-renewal notice under thirty (30) days, customer termination for convenience without payment of fees through the current term, or evergreen terms with no exit.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Non-renewal notice of thirty (30) days or more; breach termination with 30-day cure.
- **YELLOW**: Customer termination for convenience with fees paid through the current term.
- **RED**: Non-renewal notice under thirty (30) days, or termination for convenience without fee protection.
