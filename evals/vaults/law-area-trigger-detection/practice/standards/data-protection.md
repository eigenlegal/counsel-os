# Data Protection

## Our Position

**Our standard:** Vendors receiving user personal data sign our data protection addendum: processing limited to our instructions, breach notice within 48 hours of discovery, deletion on termination, security exhibit attached. Heightened terms apply when the data includes children's information — the addendum must carry the confidentiality, security, and integrity assurances applicable law requires of service providers.

**We'll accept:** Vendor-paper data terms that meet or exceed the same substance.

**We won't accept:** Vendor use of user data for its own purposes, or data terms that fall short of legal floors for children's data.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Our addendum (or equivalent) executed; security exhibit attached.
- **YELLOW**: Substantively equivalent vendor terms; security by reference.
- **RED**: No data protection terms, vendor own-use rights, or terms below legal floors.
