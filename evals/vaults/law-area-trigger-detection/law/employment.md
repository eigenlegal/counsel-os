# Employment

## Trigger Conditions

Load this area when a matter involves: employees, contractors, hiring, termination, offer letters, employment agreements, compensation, benefits, workplace policies, non-competes, worker classification.

## Key Constraints

- **Worker classification:** misclassifying employees as independent contractors creates wage-and-hour, tax, and benefits exposure; apply the economic-realities and ABC tests for the relevant states.
- **Non-compete limits:** several states (notably California) void employee non-competes; condition any restrictive covenant on the governing state's enforceability rules.
- **Wage payment timing:** state wage statutes set hard deadlines for final pay on termination; contract terms cannot waive them.

## Consequence

Wage-and-hour class exposure, state agency penalties, and back-tax assessments for misclassification.
