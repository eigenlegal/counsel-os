# Indemnification

## Our Position

**Our standard:** Mutual indemnification limited to third-party claims arising from (a) IP infringement, (b) gross negligence or willful misconduct, and (c) breach of confidentiality. Indemnification obligations are subject to the limitation of liability except for IP infringement, which carries a 2x super-cap.

**We'll accept:** A one-way indemnity from us for IP infringement claims only, capped at the 2x super-cap, where we are the vendor.

**We won't accept:** Indemnification for "any breach of this Agreement" (converts every contract claim into an uncapped indemnity), or uncapped indemnification of any kind without General Counsel approval.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Mutual, enumerated triggers, capped (with IP super-cap).
- **YELLOW**: One-way against us but enumerated and capped.
- **RED**: "Any breach" triggers, uncapped obligations, or indemnification excluded from the liability cap without enumeration.
