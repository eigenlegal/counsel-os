# Data Protection

## Our Position

**Our standard:** Vendors and processors notify us of a personal data breach within five (5) business days of discovery. When we act as processor, we commit to notifying the controller within five (5) business days of discovery. Standard confidentiality and security exhibit required.

**We'll accept:** Notice windows up to ten (10) business days for low-sensitivity data categories.

**We won't accept:** No breach notification obligation at all.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Breach notice within five (5) business days; security exhibit attached.
- **YELLOW**: Breach notice between five (5) and ten (10) business days; security terms by reference.
- **RED**: No breach notification provision, or notice longer than ten (10) business days.

## Practical Guidance

Most counterparties accept the five-business-day window without negotiation. Push for a defined "discovery" trigger rather than "confirmation" to avoid indefinite delay arguments.
