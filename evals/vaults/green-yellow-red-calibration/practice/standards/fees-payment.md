# Fees & Payment

## Our Position

**Our standard:** Net forty-five (45) days or longer from invoice date. As the customer we run a consolidated payables cycle across thousands of purchase orders; net 45 is the default our treasury operations support.

**We'll accept:** Net twenty-one (21) to net forty-four (44) days where the vendor offers an early-payment discount of at least 1.5%.

**We won't accept:** Net twenty (20) days or shorter, prepayment requirements, or late charges above 1% per month — terms our payables cycle cannot reliably meet, which converts ordinary processing time into breach and late-fee exposure.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Net forty-five (45) days or longer.
- **YELLOW**: Net twenty-one (21) to net forty-four (44) days.
- **RED**: Net twenty (20) days or shorter, any prepayment requirement, or late charges above 1% per month.
