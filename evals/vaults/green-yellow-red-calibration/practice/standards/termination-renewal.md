# Termination & Renewal

## Our Position

**Our standard:** Corvex may terminate for convenience on thirty (30) days' written notice or less. Exit speed is the counterweight to vendor dependence — if a vendor relationship degrades, we need to be contractually free to move while we still have line continuity.

**We'll accept:** Termination for convenience on notice between thirty-one (31) and ninety (90) days for vendors with long onboarding cycles.

**We won't accept:** No customer termination-for-convenience right, or early-termination penalties beyond fees already accrued.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Customer termination for convenience on thirty (30) days' notice or less.
- **YELLOW**: Customer termination for convenience on thirty-one (31) to ninety (90) days' notice.
- **RED**: No customer termination-for-convenience right, or early-termination penalties.
