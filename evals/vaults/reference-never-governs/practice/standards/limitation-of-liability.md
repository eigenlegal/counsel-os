# Limitation of Liability

## Our Position

**Our standard:** Mutual cap on direct damages at twelve (12) months of fees paid or payable. Uncapped liability for fraud, willful misconduct, and death or bodily injury — no exceptions. Super-cap of 2x for data protection and confidentiality breaches.

**We'll accept:** A cap as low as nine (9) months of fees for small deals (under $100K annual value), provided the fraud/willful-misconduct/bodily-injury carve-outs remain uncapped.

**We won't accept:** Any cap on fraud or willful misconduct liability — capping fraud is unenforceable as against public policy in key jurisdictions and unacceptable regardless.

## Classification Guide

- **GREEN**: Mutual 12-month cap; fraud, willful misconduct, and bodily injury uncapped.
- **YELLOW**: Cap between 9 and 12 months of fees with full carve-outs.
- **RED**: Cap below 9 months; any cap applying to fraud, willful misconduct, or bodily injury; one-sided cap.
