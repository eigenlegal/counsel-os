# Services Agreement — Liability Sample

Sample limitation-of-liability article from a vendor-side master services form:

> **Limitation of Liability.** EACH PARTY'S TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT, REGARDLESS OF THE THEORY OF LIABILITY AND INCLUDING CLAIMS BASED ON FRAUD, WILLFUL MISCONDUCT, OR GROSS NEGLIGENCE, SHALL NOT EXCEED THE FEES PAID BY CUSTOMER IN THE SIX (6) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM. IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.

Drafting notes from the source form: the all-inclusive cap (sweeping fraud and willful misconduct inside) paired with a short six-month lookback is presented in the form as a "clean, simple" structure that avoids carve-out negotiations.
