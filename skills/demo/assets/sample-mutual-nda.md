This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date by and between **Northwind Robotics, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation ("Northwind"), and **Vantage Systems, LLC**, a California limited liability company ("Vantage"). Northwind and Vantage are each a "Party" and together the "Parties."

## 1. Purpose

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship (the "Purpose") and, in connection with the Purpose, each Party may disclose certain confidential information to the other.

## 2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means information that is disclosed in writing and conspicuously marked "Confidential" at the time of disclosure. Information disclosed orally or visually is protected only if the disclosing Party reduces it to a writing marked "Confidential" and delivers that writing to the receiving Party within five (5) business days of the original disclosure.

## 3. Obligations

Each Party agrees to use the other Party's Confidential Information solely for the Purpose and to protect it using the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information. Each Party may disclose Confidential Information to its employees, affiliates, and contractors who need to know it for the Purpose.

## 4. Residuals

Notwithstanding anything to the contrary, each Party may use for any purpose the ideas, concepts, know-how, and techniques related to the other Party's Confidential Information that are retained in the unaided memory of its personnel, including for the development, marketing, and sale of products and services.

## 5. Term

This Agreement, and each Party's obligations with respect to Confidential Information, shall remain in effect for one (1) year from the Effective Date, after which the receiving Party shall have no further obligation with respect to the Confidential Information.

## 6. Return of Materials

Upon written request by the disclosing Party, the receiving Party will use commercially reasonable efforts to return or destroy the disclosing Party's Confidential Information.

## 7. Compelled Disclosure

If a receiving Party is required by law, regulation, or legal process to disclose any Confidential Information, it may do so without liability under this Agreement.

## 8. No License

Nothing in this Agreement grants either Party any license or right to the other Party's Confidential Information except the limited right to use it for the Purpose.

## 9. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles.
