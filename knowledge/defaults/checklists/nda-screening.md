# NDA Screening Checklist

## Initial Classification
- [ ] Identify NDA type: mutual or one-way (disclosing/receiving)
- [ ] Confirm the NDA type matches the business need
- [ ] Identify the business purpose and requesting stakeholder
- [ ] Check if we have an existing NDA with this counterparty
- [ ] Determine if our template should be proposed instead

## Party Information
- [ ] Counterparty legal entity name verified
- [ ] Jurisdiction of incorporation confirmed
- [ ] Authorized signatory identified
- [ ] Affiliate coverage addressed (does the NDA cover subsidiaries?)

## Critical Terms — Rapid Triage
- [ ] **Mutual vs. One-Way:** Direction matches our needs
- [ ] **Definition of Confidential Information:** Broad enough to cover oral, written, electronic, and visual disclosures
- [ ] **Marking Requirements:** No overly restrictive marking/labeling requirements that could create gaps
- [ ] **Permitted Use:** Limited to the stated business purpose
- [ ] **Duration:** Between 2-5 years with trade secret carve-out for perpetual protection
- [ ] **Standard Exclusions:** Public domain, prior knowledge, independent development, rightful third-party disclosure
- [ ] **Residuals Clause:** Absent or narrowly drafted (flag if permits use of retained ideas/concepts)

## Non-Standard Provisions — Red Flags
- [ ] No non-solicitation or non-compete clauses (unless specifically required)
- [ ] No standstill provisions (M&A context only)
- [ ] No exclusivity obligations
- [ ] No IP assignment or broad license grants
- [ ] No indemnification obligations (unusual for NDAs)
- [ ] No liquidated damages provisions
- [ ] No non-disparagement clause
- [ ] No audit or inspection rights (unusual for NDAs)

## Protective Provisions
- [ ] Return or destruction of confidential information upon request or termination
- [ ] Certification of destruction available upon request
- [ ] Compelled disclosure permitted with prior notice and cooperation
- [ ] Injunctive relief acknowledged as an available remedy
- [ ] Need-to-know dissemination limits (employees, contractors, advisors)
- [ ] Binding confidentiality obligations for permitted recipients

## Legal and Administrative
- [ ] Governing law and jurisdiction acceptable
- [ ] Dispute resolution mechanism reasonable
- [ ] Assignment restrictions present
- [ ] Term of the agreement (not just confidentiality period) defined
- [ ] Amendment and waiver provisions included
- [ ] Counterparts and electronic signature permitted

## Approval and Execution
- [ ] Classified as FAST TRACK / MINOR EDITS / FULL REVIEW
- [ ] If MINOR EDITS: changes identified and redline prepared
- [ ] If FULL REVIEW: issues list prepared with risk classifications
- [ ] Signing authority confirmed per delegation of authority
- [ ] NDA logged in contract management system with key metadata
- [ ] Business stakeholder notified of execution
