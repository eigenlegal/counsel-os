[How much risk are you willing to accept? This calibrates GREEN/YELLOW/RED thresholds]

Choose one and elaborate:
- **Conservative**: Minimize all risk. Flag aggressively. Prefer walking away over accepting unfavorable terms.
- **Moderate**: Accept reasonable commercial risk. Focus energy on material issues. Standard market terms are generally acceptable.
- **Aggressive**: Move fast, accept more risk for speed. Only escalate true deal-breakers. Comfortable with asymmetric terms if the business case is strong.

## Prioritization Framework
[When you can't negotiate everything, what do you fight for first?]

Example:
> 1. IP ownership and assignment — non-negotiable
> 2. Data protection and breach notification — must meet regulatory minimums
> 3. Limitation of liability — must be mutual and proportionate
> 4. Indemnification — resist uncapped, one-sided indemnification
> 5. Termination rights — need reasonable exit paths
> 6. Everything else — negotiate if time permits, otherwise accept market terms
