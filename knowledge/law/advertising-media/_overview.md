## Overview

# Advertising & Media Law

## Trigger Conditions

Load this area when the matter involves ANY of:
- Advertising, marketing campaigns, promotional materials
- Media production, content licensing, distribution agreements
- Endorsements, testimonials, influencer marketing
- Right of publicity, personality rights, likeness
- Defamation, libel, slander, false light
- Section 230, platform liability, content moderation
- Content creation, user-generated content, syndication

## Interaction with Other Areas

- **consumer-protection/**: FTC Act Section 5 applies to deceptive advertising; endorsement guides overlap with consumer protection enforcement
- **ip-and-technology/**: Copyright in content, trademark in advertising, trade dress, domain disputes
- **data-privacy/**: AdTech data collection, behavioral advertising, COPPA for child-directed content
- **employment/**: Talent agreements, union requirements (SAG-AFTRA), independent contractor vs. employee for content creators
