# White Collar & Investigations

## Trigger Conditions

Load this area when the matter involves:
- **Keywords**: investigation, whistleblower, compliance program, DOJ, SEC enforcement, fraud, FCPA enforcement, monitor, DPA, NPA, qui tam
- **Document types**: subpoenas, document holds, investigation reports, compliance program assessments, cooperation agreements, deferred prosecution agreements, whistleblower complaints, SAR filings
- **Scenarios**: internal investigation, government inquiry, whistleblower complaint, compliance program evaluation, voluntary self-disclosure, enforcement action response, monitor engagement
