## Overview

# Environmental & ESG Law

## Trigger Conditions

This area applies when the matter involves any of the following:
- **Keywords**: environmental, ESG, climate, sustainability, emissions, CERCLA, remediation, green, carbon, disclosure, net-zero, Scope 1, Scope 2, Scope 3
- **Document types**: environmental compliance agreements, sustainability reports, ESG disclosures, environmental indemnities, remediation agreements, carbon offset contracts
- **Transaction types**: acquisitions involving environmental liabilities, ESG-linked financing, climate risk assessments, sustainability-linked supply agreements
