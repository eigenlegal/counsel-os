## Overview

# Labor Relations Law

## Trigger Conditions

Load this area when the matter involves:
- **Keywords**: union, NLRA, collective bargaining, labor relations, NLRB, unfair labor practice, strike, lockout, organizing, protected activity
- **Document types**: collective bargaining agreements, union contracts, NLRB charges, arbitration awards, grievance filings, neutrality agreements, union authorization cards, election notices
- **Scenarios**: union organizing campaign, collective bargaining negotiation, unfair labor practice charge, strike or lockout, grievance arbitration, successor employer obligations, management campaign rules

## Sub-Topic Triggers

| Sub-Topic File | Load When |
|---|---|
| `nlra.md` | NLRA rights, Section 7, Section 8 ULPs, NLRB jurisdiction, employee exclusions |
| `collective-bargaining.md` | Contract negotiation, mandatory/permissive subjects, impasse, successor obligations |
| `union-organizing.md` | Organizing campaign, election petition, employer rules (TIPS), card check, Cemex |
| `strikes-lockouts.md` | Economic/ULP strike, lockout, picketing, replacement workers, secondary activity |

## Interaction with Other Areas

- **Employment** (`employment/`): employee classification, at-will exceptions, wage/hour for union employees
- **Antitrust** (`antitrust/`): labor exemption from antitrust, multi-employer bargaining
- **Consumer Protection** (`consumer-protection/`): secondary boycott consumer publicity
- **Corporate** (`corporate/`): successor obligations in M&A, labor due diligence
